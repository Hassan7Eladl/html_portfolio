My Best Anime List

The Top 3

Attack on titans

Attack on Titan is a dark fantasy anime where humanity fights for survival against giant man-eating Titans.
The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they uncover shocking truths about their world.
It stands out for its deep themes, complex characters, and unpredictable plot twists.Stunning animation and epic music enhance the intense storytelling.
It's widely praised as one of the best anime of all time.

Death Note

Death Note is a psychological thriller anime about a genius student, Light Yagami, who finds a notebook that kills anyone whose name is written in it.
He aims to create a crime-free world as the god-like “Kira,” but is hunted by the brilliant detective L.
The anime explores themes of justice, morality, and power. It’s praised for its smart writing, intense mind games, and deep character conflict.

Black Clover

Black Clover is a fantasy action anime about Asta, a boy born without magic in a world where magic is everything. Despite this, he vows to become the Wizard King, using a rare anti-magic power and relentless determination.Alongside his rival Yuno, he battles dark forces and uncovers secrets about their world.
It’s known for its fast-paced fights, underdog story, and strong character growth.
